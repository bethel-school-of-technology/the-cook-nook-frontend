Cook Nook

Welcome to Cook Nook!

How to use this app:

  1. First find your favorite recipes.
  2. Then press the Add Recipe button and fill the fields provided.
  3. Click save. Now your recipe is recorded on Cook Nook! You can edit, delete, and add more recipes if you wish!

Why we made this app:

Through many generations, cook books were passed down from parent to child. As time goes on, those cook books begin to fall apart, and with internet being a common luxury, it's often easy to forget those old recipes. Here at Cook Nook, we seek to preserve the recipes in a twenty first century way, ensuring that you never lose your family's scrumptous recipes that no internet guru could ever replace!

Why it's important:

Food isn't just a requirement of living; it's our culture and family traditions. It's in our holidays and our birthdays, football games and cook outs, weddings and anniversaries. Food is a large part of who we are, and brings families and friends together for every occasion, and can remind us of our memories made with each other.
We want to help preserve these slices of life with our online cook book, Cook Nook.
